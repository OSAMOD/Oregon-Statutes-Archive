# Oregon Statutes Archive (OSA)

The Oregon Statutes Archive (OSA) is a public, non-commercial project dedicated to preserving and presenting the Oregon Revised Statutes (ORS) in accessible, structured formats.

This repository exists to improve readability, reference use, and long-term preservation of Oregon statutory law. The content is organized for clarity and navigation while preserving the original statutory language.

## Purpose

- Provide an accessible, structured archive of the Oregon Revised Statutes  
- Improve readability without altering statutory meaning  
- Support public reference, education, and research  
- Preserve historical and current statutory material in open formats  

OSA is not a legal service and does not provide legal advice or interpretation.

## Scope

This repository contains:
- Statutory text derived from the Oregon Revised Statutes  
- Organizational and formatting work to improve navigation and reference  
- Supporting documentation related to structure and archival methodology  

No commentary, advocacy, or legal analysis is provided unless explicitly labeled.

## Source Material

The Oregon Revised Statutes are public domain materials published by the State of Oregon.  
OSA does not claim ownership over the statutory text itself.
